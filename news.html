"Ridhi Gutta: The Teen Scientist Rewriting the Rules of Global Health"

August 4th, 2025

At just a few years into high school, Ridhi Gutta had already stumbled into the epicenter of a medical mystery that would ignite her life’s work. From uncovering her hometown’s link to an Ebola outbreak to founding two global biotech initiatives before turning eighteen, Ridhi’s story is anything but typical. She’s redefining what it means to be young in science—writing vaccine code from her bedroom, influencing EU biotech policy, and challenging the inequities that decide which lives get saved first. Her journey is a blueprint for resilience, innovation, and refusing to wait for permission.
