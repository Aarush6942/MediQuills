About MediQuills
Where Young Minds Meet Science, One Page at a Time

MediQuills is a Virginia based nonprofit organization dedicated to enhancing scientific literacy among youth. Founded in 2024, MediQuills has already made a profound impact with four of our books reaching 'Top New Release Worldwide' status and 1 book reaching Top 10 Bestseller Worldwide status out of 32.8M+ books internationally. MediQuills has impacted over 10,000 individuals worldwide with sales in over 10 countries and 4 continents.

Children's Book Series

Our book series is a powerful platform created by and for the next generation of changemakers. Combining science, storytelling, and real-world impact, we bring together young authors, researchers, and innovators from around the globe to tackle urgent issues—from medicine and mental health to climate change and technology. Each book is written with clarity, passion, and purpose—designed to inspire, inform, and ignite action.

With voices as diverse as our readers, we believe that anyone can be an author of change. Whether you're a teen curious about the world or a parent looking to spark your child's curiosity, our books are crafted to make complex topics accessible and exciting.

To date, we've published multiple international bestsellers, reached over 10,000 readers, and collaborated with thought leaders, educators, and organizations worldwide. But we're just getting started.

Vaccine Policy Initiative

Our Vaccine Policy Initiative is a youth-powered, science-driven movement reimagining the future of immunization through bold policy change. At the intersection of public health, advocacy, and equity, we are committed to fighting misinformation, improving vaccine access, and ensuring that lifesaving science reaches every corner of the world.

We've published 4 official policy papers with the European Union, tackling urgent issues ranging from global vaccine equity to youth-led pandemic preparedness. Our work translates complex scientific data into actionable, people-centered policy—and proves that young people belong in the rooms where decisions are made.

With a reach of over 10,000 individuals globally, we combine original research, youth consultation, and expert collaboration to create a platform where science meets strategy, and voices often left out of public health policy are finally heard.

Inspirational Speakers

Our Inspirational Speakers series brings science, storytelling, and activism to life—one conversation at a time. We spotlight changemakers, medical pioneers, teen researchers, entrepreneurs, and advocates from around the world who are rewriting what's possible in medicine, policy, and innovation.

Hosted across Instagram Live and reels, our platform makes powerful conversations accessible to everyone, not just the people in lecture halls or conference rooms. From youth leaders breaking barriers in STEM to public health heroes on the frontlines, our guests share raw, real stories designed to inspire action and empower the next generation.

We've built a fast-growing digital community through @mediquills and our partner platforms—reaching thousands with every episode. Whether you're tuning in to spark your next big idea or find your voice in science, our speakers are proof that age is never a barrier to impact.
