Welcome to MediQuills
Where Young Minds Meet Science, One Page at a Time

MediQuills is a Virginia-based nonprofit organization dedicated to enhancing scientific literacy among youth.

    Our Mission

    MediQuills, having published four #1 release children's books out of 32.8M+ books internationally, is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to enhancing scientific literacy among youth. We aim to provide underprivileged children access to scientific knowledge and promote vaccine access through public policy initiatives with the European Union and United Nations.

    What we plan to do

    • Platform created by and for the next generation of changemakers
    • Combines science, storytelling, and real-world impact
    • Topics range from medicine and mental health to climate change and technology
    • Books are written with clarity, passion, and purpose to inspire and inform
    • Published multiple international bestsellers
    • Reached over 10,000 readers in collaboration with global educators and leaders

    Make a difference

      Join us in making a real difference—your time and skills can help inspire the next generation of changemakers. At MediQuills, every volunteer plays a vital role in bringing science, creativity, and opportunity to children worldwide. You can write a book, start a new chapter, and many more! Together, we can turn curiosity into lifelong learning and hope into action.

    What are we doing now?

    Right now, we’re focused on inspiring young people through science education and storytelling. We’re expanding our programs and collaborating with communities to promote scientific literacy and health awareness. Together, we’re building a future where youth have the knowledge and voice to create positive change. Learn more by checking our news page.
