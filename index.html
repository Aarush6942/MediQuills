About MediQuills

MediQuills, having published four #1 release children's books out of 32.8M+ books internationally, is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to enhancing scientific literacy among youth. We aim to provide underprivileged children access to scientific knowledge and promote vaccine access through public policy initiatives with the European Union and United Nations.
